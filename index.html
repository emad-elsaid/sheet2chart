Google sheet to a timeline chart

Enter your google sheet ID to convert it to a timeline chart

The google sheet must be public and must have have these 3 columns:
  1. Position: String for timeline row that record belongs to
  2. Name: A label for that row on the timeline
  3. Start: A date for the beginning of that row in timeline
  4. End: a date for the end of that row in timeline
An example could be found here https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/19AoR5siwI-cbrlJN47cZV7nhoZ9mO76XBUAWtO5Xb8A/edit#gid=0
